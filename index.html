Juliette Salom

Juliette Salom (she/her) is a writer from Naarm/Melbourne. Currently completing a Bachelor of Arts (Creative Writing) with a minor in Cinema Studies at RMIT University, Juliette spends her time writing fiction, articles and stories for the screen. Her work has been published in Ramona Magazine, Catalyst, Swim Meet Lit Mag, and she is also a frequent contributor to She's Eco.

Artist Statement

Juliette's work aims to entertain, engage, and ask questions, but mainly just to have a little fun.
